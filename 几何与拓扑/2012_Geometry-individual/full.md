5. Let $x : M \to \mathbb { R } ^ { 3 }$ be a closed surface in 3-dimensional Euclidean space. Its Gaussian curvature and mean curvature are denoted by $K$ and $H$ respectively. Prove that:

$$

$$

$$
g = \frac {\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} (d x ^ {i}) ^ {2}}{[ 1 + \frac {c}{4} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} (x ^ {i}) ^ {2} ] ^ {2}}
